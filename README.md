# Threadline — Adaptive Fashion Market Intelligence Pipeline

**Course:** INFO7375 Branding & AI  
**Student:** Raksha Krishna Moorthy | Northeastern University  

## What is Threadline?
Threadline is a market intelligence pipeline that identifies specific functional garment needs of patients with conditions like post-mastectomy breast cancer recovery, arthritis, ostomy, and diabetes — and finds where separate conditions share one need that a single product could address.
